Research
×

We are a full-stack computing and semiconductor engineering company specializing in streamlining the process of interpretation of neural signals from the brain through our integrated AI development platform.

With SyncMind’s integrated Neural Platform, neuroscientists and neurosurgeons can accelerate research and diagnosis of neural ailments, pave the way for advanced neuro-electronic therapy and bio-electronics interface applications.

We bring together a community of engineers, mathematicians, cryptographers, data scientists, geneticists and neuroscientists to create a reliable, secure and cutting edge platform to extend the reaches of our understanding of the human brain.

We build full-stack computing platforms for Neural Signal Processing, Deep Brain Stimulation, and AI for brain-computer interface applications.

We’re integrating advanced architectures in Memory, Processing Systems, Compilation tool-chain, Kernel, Architectural and Microelectronics layers of our integrated platform for neural signal processing and inference.

We welcome talented engineers and developers in our team to work with us across all the layers of the computing stack.
