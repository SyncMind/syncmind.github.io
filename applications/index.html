The brain-computer interface market was valued at USD 1.58 Billion in 2020 and is expected to reach USD 3.21 Billion by 2026.

OUR BUSINESS MODEL



OUR CUSTOMERS
We are developing an integrated Neural Platform which would aid neuroscientists and neurosurgeons accelerate research and diagnosis of neural ailments, pave the way for advanced neuro-electronic therapy and develop new applications of bio-electronics interfaces. Our primary goal is to develop computation structures to address applications in Medical Care, Speech and Body Function Restoration, Deep Brain Stimulation, Research applications for patients with Dementia, Alzheimer's, and many other Psychological Ailments.

In the short term, our customers are
    Neuroscience Research Institutes for real-time time-series signal processing.
    Diagnostic Departments at Hospitals and Animal Labs.
    Licensing of firmware components and IC standard cells to electronics manufacturers.

In the long term,
    Personalized Neural Processing Integrated System for human patients.
    Applications beyond Neural Signal Processing in ML Hardware development:
      1. Wearable Electronics,
      2. Power Sensitive Controllers in Industry (such as, cryogenic systems),
      3. BCI control,
      4. Brain-interactive Augmented Reality
          etc.
